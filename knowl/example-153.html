Example 2.3.25.

For each of the following functions, use the appropriate formulas to find the derivative. Then, find an equation for the tangent line to the function at the given point.

  1. \(S(p) = \sqrt{p}-p\) at \((9,-6)\)

  2. \(F(r) = \dfrac{5}{r^3}\) at \((-2,-5/8)\)

  3. \(A(r) = \pi r^2\) at \((1,\pi)\)

  4. \(F(y) = \lrpar{\dfrac{1}{y^2}-\dfrac{3}{y^4}}(y+5y^3)\) at \((-1,12)\)

  5. \(J(v) = (v^3-2v)(v^{-4}+v^{-2})\) at \((1,-4)\)

  6. \(f(x) = \dfrac{1+2x}{3-4x}\) at \(\lrpar{\dfrac{1}{2},1}\)

  7. \(Y(t)=\dfrac{1}{t^3+2t^2-1}\) at \(\lrpar{\dfrac{1}{2},-\dfrac{8}{3}}\)

  8. \(h(u) = \dfrac{(u+2)^2}{1-u}\) at \((2,-16)\)

  9. \(f(t) = \dfrac{\sqrt[3]{t}}{t-3}\) at \(\lrpar{8,\dfrac{2}{5}}\)
